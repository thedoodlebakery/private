Solavei Mobile Service also includes unlimited international texting and MMS picture message at no additional charge!

Q: How Can I login in to my account?
A:
  1. Visit solavei.com and log in using your unique username and password.
  2. Select International Long Distance from the Dashboard.
  3. Type your ten digit registered phone number
  4. Enter your passcode (sent to you via text message when you signed up with Solavei International Long Distance)
Q: What is a passcode and why do I need one?
A: A passcode is a unique four-digit number assigned to your Solavei ILD account. The purpose of this number is to protect you in two ways:
  1. Help you securely log into your account online
  2. Protect all account information included credit card information.

Q: How do I get my passcode?
A: After you register for Solavei ILD, a text message and/or email containing your unique four-digit password will be sent to you.

Q: I forgot my passcode. How can I retrieve it?
A: You many retrieve your passcode by visiting www.ild.solavei.com. Click the "Sign In" button located under the Solavei logo and then click the "Retrieve Passcode" link and enter your registered phone number on the next screen. A text message and/or email will be sent to you with your unique four-digit passcode.

Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, go to www.ild.solavei.com and click the "Sign Up" tab located under the Solavei logo. Follow the easy instructions and in a few steps you will be ready to use Solavei ILD!

Q: Do I need a credit card?
A: In order to sign up for an account or recharge your account through www.ild.solavei.com, you will need a valid credit card.

Q: How do I add funds to my account?
A:
  1. Sign in to your account
  2. Click the "Add Funds" button
  3. Enter the desired amount, credit card number and CCV number
  4. Click "Submit" to recharge your account

Use auto-recharge so you are never caught without ILD talk time. Auto-Recharge is easily switched on and off. Remember, when purchasing with a credit card, there is a money back guarantee for credit balances remaining on an account.

Q: Why was my credit card declined?
A: There are a few reasons why your credit card may be declined.
  1. Your credit card on file may have expired or have insufficient available balance.
  2. The billing address you provided may not match the billing address on file with your credit card company.

Please review your credit card and personal information to ensure all information is accurate and try your order again. If you are still not successful, please call your credit card company's customer service.

Q: Can I safely enter my credit card number on your website?
A: Yes, all of our members' personal information is stored safely behind our firewall and all online transactions are handled with the highest level of security. We incorporate Microsoft SSL (Security Socket Layer) into our website and employ the latest encryption technology to prevent our members' information from being intercepted.

Q: Which access number should I use?
A: Most calling plans allow you to call anywhere in the U.S. at the same price. If you are unsure about your plan, please check with your provider to ensure that you will not incur additional costs. Alternatively, for an extra 1.5 cents per minute you can use the toll free access numbers.

Q: Do I need a long distance carrier on my phone to use Solavei ILD service?
A: No, you do not need a long distance carrier on your phone to use this service.

Q: How do I make calls from a non-registered phone number?
A:
  1. Dial the access number
  2. Enter your registered phone number when prompted
  3. You will then be prompted to register the non-registered phone number or to continue with your call

Q: How do I add additional phone numbers to my account?
A: Sign into your account and click the "Main" tab. Click "Add Registered #" in the "Registered Numbers" section to add up to nine additional phone numbers to your account.
