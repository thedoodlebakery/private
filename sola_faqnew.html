Support page with ILD FAQs

Solavei International Long Distance is a great prepaid add-on to Solavei Mobile Service that lets members call landline and mobile numbers in more than 200 countries at competitive rates by purchasing minutes in $10 increments. Solavei Mobile Service also includes unlimited international texting and MMS picture message at no additional charge!

Q: How do I create an account?

A:

To create an account, go to www.ild.solavei.com and click the “Sign Up” tab located under the Solavei logo. Follow the easy instructions and click Create Account. You will immediately receive a text message and phone call from an unknown number on the registered phone number you entered at ild.solavei.com. You must answer the phone and follow the IVR prompts to enter the 4 digit passcode that was sent to you via text message. Once you have completed the final step of activation, you are ready to use your Solavei ILD!

Q. What if I miss the call from the unknown number when going through the Account Creation process?

A:

Go to www.ild.solavei.com - at the bottom right of the page under Is your Account Not Activated click the blue Call Me to Activate button. Enter the phone number used at registration and click the Create Account button. You will immediately receive another phone call to complete the activation process.

Q: How Can I login in to my account?

A:

  1. Visit solavei.com and log in using your unique username and password.
  2. Select International Long Distance from the Dashboard.
  3. Type your ten digit registered phone number.
  4. Enter your passcode (sent to you via text message when you signed up with Solavei International Long Distance)

Q: What is a passcode and why do I need one?

A:

A passcode is a unique four-digit number assigned to your Solavei ILD account. The purpose of this number is to protect you in two ways:

  1. Help you securely log into your account online
  2. Protect all account information included credit card information.

Q: How do I get my passcode?

A:

After you register for Solavei ILD, a text message and/or email containing your unique four-digit password will be sent to you.

Q: I forgot my passcode. How can I retrieve it?

A:

You many retrieve your passcode by visiting www.ild.solavei.coma>. Click the “Sign In” button located under the Solavei logo and then click the “Retrieve Passcode” link and enter your registered phone number on the next screen. A text message and/or email will be sent to you with your unique four-digit passcode.

Q: How do I update my account information?

A:

  1. Log into your Solavei ILD account
  2. Click Profile
  3. Click on the “edit profile” button
  4. Edit your information
  5. Click “Update”

Q. How can I check my available balance?

When you place an international call, you will hear one of two messages: the available balance in either dollars or minutes, or an indication that the balance is low and needs to be refilled.

Q: How do I add funds to my account?

A:

  1. Sign in to your account
  2. Click the “Add Funds” button
  3. Enter the desired amount, credit card number and CCV number
  4. Click “Submit” to recharge your account Use auto-recharge so you are never caught without ILD talk time. Auto-Recharge is easily switched on and off. Remember, when purchasing with a credit card, there is a money back guarantee for credit balances remaining on an account.

Q: Do I need a long distance carrier on my phone to use Solavei ILD service?

A:

No, you do not need a long distance carrier on your phone to use this service.

Q: How do I make calls from a non-registered phone number?

A:

  1. Dial the access number
  2. Enter your registered phone number when prompted
  3. You will then be prompted to register the non-registered phone number or to continue with your call

Q: How do I add additional phone numbers to my account?

A:

Sign into your account and click the “Main” tab. Click “Add Registered #” in the “Registered Numbers” section to add up to nine additional phone numbers to your account.

Q: Why was my credit card declined?

A:

There are a few reasons why your credit card may be declined.

  1. Your credit card on file may have expired or have insufficient available balance.
  2. The billing address you provided may not match the billing address on file with your credit card company.
  3. Please review your credit card and personal information to ensure all information is accurate and try your order again. If you are still not successful, please call your credit card company’s customer service.

Q: Can I safely enter my credit card number on your website?

A:

Yes, all of our members’ personal information is stored safely behind our firewall and all online transactions are handled with the highest level of security. We incorporate Microsoft SSL (Security Socket Layer) into our website and employ the latest encryption technology to prevent our members’ information from being intercepted.
